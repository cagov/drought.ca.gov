State actions against drought

May 10, 2021

Governor Gavin Newsom significantly expands his April 21 drought emergency proclamation to include Klamath River, Sacramento-San Joaquin Delta and Tulare Lake Watershed counties where accelerated action is needed to protect public health, safety and the environment.

April 21, 2021

Governor Newsom issues an Emergency Proclamation to advance state drought preparation and response in the Russian River Watershed of Sonoma and Mendocino counties.

April 1, 2021

Governor Newsom directed multiple state agencies to prepare a Water Resilience Portfolio  (released in July 2020).
